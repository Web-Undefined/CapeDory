Where to Look:
Buy or Sell Your Boat

The CDSOA Inc. neither endorses nor recommends any of the online resources listed below. They are listed here for informational purposes. Any questions or problems concerning any of the online resources or their products or services should be directed to the individual online resource.

  • SAMS (Society of Marine Surveyors) -- get your boat surveyed before you buy/sell.

 

  • American Boat Listing -- Find your next boat.  ABL will advertise your boat for sale on over 40 other web sites.
  • BoatBoss.com -- Basic Ad is Free and runs for 180 days.
  • Boats.com -- Ad packages start at around $55 and includes 1 photograph.
  • Boats Depot -- Basic Ad is Free.
  • Boatsville.com -- Basic listings start at $19.95 and your ad stays online until you sell..
  • Boat Trader Online -- Magazine as well as online listings -- Basic listing starts at around $55, includes 1 photograph and runs for 4 weeks.
  • Boat U.S. Classifieds -- Magazine as well as online listings -- This site offers both free and paid classifieds. Basic Ad is Free and runs for 3 months.
  • BUCNET.com -- Contact a broker to get your boat listed.
  • Cape Dory Board -- search the "Buy/Sell CD Boats and Gear" forum right here on our own web site -- Postings are Free.
  • Craigslist -- Local classifieds and forums for 450 cities worldwide - community moderated, and largely Free.
  • EveryBoat -- Boat classifieds online. Buy or sell a new or used boat -- Listings start at around $15, includes up to 21 photos and runs until boat is sold.
  • iboats.com -- Basic listing starts at around $25 for the first month.
  • MarineSource.com -- Listings of brokered boats.
  • PassageMaker -- Listings in Passage Maker Magazine.
  • SailboatListing.com -- Basic Ads are Free. $20 to feature your listing on their homepage for up to 2 months or until your boat sells, whichever comes first!
  • Sell-a-Boat.com -- Basic Ad packages start at around $30 and run until boat is sold.
  • SellYourBoat.com -- Worldwide yacht listings -- Associated with 1001 Boats For Sale -- 4 month listings for around $25.00, which includes all set up costs, including the scanning of your photos.
  • Soundings -- Magazine as well as online listings to Sell or Buy -- Basic text listing starts at around $68 -- Ad includes both Print and Internet exposure and your ad will appear online within 24 hours of your ad being processed. Ad will run online until the 15th of the month of the print publication.
  • The Big Lot -- Listing is $150, includes photos and runs until boat is sold. No renewal fees.
  • The Marine Web -- Associated with UsedBoats.com -- Basic Ad packages start at around $40 and run until boat is sold.
  • UsedBoatYard.com -- Ad Runs until it sells! Include up to 16 photos for $27.
  • YachtWorld -- Contact a broker to get your boat listed.
  • 1001 Boats For Sale -- Worldwide yacht listings -- 4 month listings for around $25.00, which includes all set up costs, including the scanning of your photos.

Boat Haulers:

  • A1 Haul-A-Boat, Inc. -- transport broker, providing a service that connects 'haulers' with customers. A1 Haul-A-Boat is not a carrier. All haulers are independent of A1 Haul-A-Boat and carry their own insurance and bonding requirements.
  • Able Boat Transport -- can handle a variety of boat types and specialty boat moving throughout the U.S., including Hawaii and Alaska, Canada and Mexico
  • Atlantic Boat Transportation, Inc. -- Transport sailboats and powerboats up to 56 feet, 15' beam, and 35,000lbs -- operating area is mainly from the East Coast to the Mississippi River. We also transport to LA, TX, MO, IL, WI and MN.
  • BoatsExpress, Inc. -- boat transport & boat moving services available to the United States, Canada, Mexico, and 12 European Countries.
  • Boat Owner's World Directory of Boat Haulers
  • boatsshipping -- boats.com and the marine industry's leading boat transport service providers have joined forces to bring you boatsshipping.
  • Brown Transportation Company -- can arrange transport to all 50 States and foreign ports by overland and overseas delivery.
  • Brownell Systems Inc. -- services include hauling and launching of sailboats and powerboats to and from many New England boat ramps, crane service, and overland transport from points between Maine and Maryland, and some Great Lakes Areas.
  • Cross Country Boat Transport, Inc. -- can handle cradle or non-cradle power or sail boats up to 50 feet -- transports nationwide
  • DRD Motorsports -- transports all types of boat throughout the United States and Canada
  • Express Boat Transport, Corp. -- land transportation, overseas shipping, shrink wrap, cradels, and storage.
  • FindAHauler.com -- an independent third party load matching service for anyone that is involved in or needs boat transport or shipping services.

  • Slip2Shore Boat Hauling -- small, family-owned business -- transports boats nationwide
  • Stonington Marine Transport -- hauls boats on the Atlantic Coast
  • Triton Yacht Transporters -- Providing transport of sailboats and powerboats up to 60'. Serving the USA and parts of Canada. Both hydraulic and low-boy trailer service.
  • uShip Boat Transport -- The “eBay of Boat Transport”- uShip's marketplace may save you up to 80%.
  • We Haul Boats Inc. -- family-owned, not brokers.
