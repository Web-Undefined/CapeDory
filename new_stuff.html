London Goes to Sea: Restoring and Sailing an Old Boat on a Budget -- By Peter Baumgartner. “Although I had sailed for years, I had never owned a sailboat before and certainly never attempted to restore one…." The story of the restoration of an old Cape Dory 27. Order your copy today from the publisher, Sheridan House, use this form, or visit Amazon.com or Barnes & Noble.
Port Rain Shields -- Mike Cyr, in conjunction with Cape Dory owner Paul Kamins, have created a plastic rain shield to fit the standard bronze 13 1/2" Spartan portlights on your Cape Dory. These rain shields allow for greater ventilation and permit leaving the ports open during periods of rain. If interested, or to order, please contact Mike Cyr at mcyr@maine.rr.com or by calling 207-229-0195. The Port Rain Shields are $65 each. For multiple rain shields: 2 @ $62.50 each, 4 @ $60 each, 5+ @ $55 each.
Bronze Port Screens -- Cape Dory owner John Danicic has created a bronze port screen to fit the standard bronze 13 1/2" Spartan portlights on your Cape Dory. These screens allow for greater ventilation and permit leaving the ports open while keeping the insects out. If interested, or to order, please visit The Screens of Schooner Bay web site or contact John Danicic at john@johndanicic.com or by calling 612-308-2994. The Port Screens are $28 each.
Cape Dory builder's plates -- Order a new bronze hull number builder's plate for your Cape Dory. Bristol Bronze also offers a large selection of traditional bronze fittings.

Half-hull model by John Friday pictured above.

Half-Hull Models -- John Friday, fellow Cape Dory owner, CDSOA member, and model builder, will custom craft a half-hull model of your Cape Dory in 1:24 scale, handpainted to match your CD, and mounted on a varnished hardwood board. Every hull is identified with a brass plaque affixed to the board and engraved with the vessel's description and name. Order Form (Microsoft Word or PDF).

Some other sources: Trident Studio and The Scale Model Company. Both businesses have some historical relationsip to the original suppliers of the CD half hulls, and both have an extensive list of CD models on their web sites. And Custom Half Models by Todd Dunn.

Line Drawings: We have the line drawings for only two Cape Dory sailboat models, the CD27 and CD28.
