2017 General Events

CDSOA 21st ANNUAL MEETING

Mystic, CT
September 16, 2017
Please register by September 5th!

Click Here for additional information, including the registration form.

Members of the Cape Dory Sailboat Owners Association, Inc. are cordially invited to attend the
21st Annual Meeting & Barbecue Dinner in historic, Mystic, CT.

The 2017 Annual Membership Meeting will be held on Saturday, September 16th, in Mystic Connecticut. Mark your calendars! You may want to make it the entire weekend as there's lots to see and do.

We have secured the local VFW hall for our festivities. Our meal will be catered by Chester's Barbecue. And this year's speaker is fellow Cape Dory owner and Northeast Fleet member Lisa Goodwin.

This venue boasts many outstanding attractions such as Mystic Seaport, the Mystic Aquarium, Olde Mistick Village etc.

Several marinas are nearby should you wish to arrive by boat, and the busy downtown area is easily reached by auto directly off I-95 or U.S. Rte. 1. Airports in the immediate area include Bradley International (BDL) and Groton-New London Airport (KGON) in Connecticut, and T. F. Green Airport (PVD) in Rhode Island.

The Amtrak station in Mystic is within walking distance of the marinas, the VFW and the Seaport..

Since this is a tourist destination both lodging and slip rental are best arranged well in advance, hence this preliminary advisory.

To register, click this link then print the PDF document, complete the registration form in ink, then mail it with your check to Jim Walsh. Please register by September 5th.
