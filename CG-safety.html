Other Resources:

Navigation and Weather:

  • BoatU.S.
    • Hurricane Resource Center
    • BoatU.S. Foundation
  • CoastalBoating.net -- many resources for cruisers including weather, navbigation and safety
  • Jenifer Clark's Gulfstream -- this service is not free, but Jennifer provides realtime ocean charts and Gulf Stream data which is invaluable to cruisers.
  • Johns Hopkins University Applied Physics Laboratory / Ocean Remote Sensing Group -- near real-time presentation of sea surface temperature imagery
  • Latitude/Longitude Distance Calculation -- determine the distance between two points on the earth given their latitudes and longitudes.
  • Maptech.com -- the creators of the Embassy Guides, ChartKits and navigation software. Also provides an online mapserver which includes nautical, terrain and aeronautical charts.
    • NGA List of Lights
    • Sight Reduction Tables for Marine Navigation
    • USCG Light List
  • NWS Marine Product Dissemination Information -- Worldwide weather fax frequencies, schedules, forecasts, radiofax, satellites and other products.
  • Rutgers University Coastal Ocean Observation Lab -- real-time satellite data (the main area of coverage is NJ but there is satellite data for all of the eastern U.S. and the Great Lakes.
  • Surface Distance Between Two Points of Latitude and Longitude AND Great Circle Distances Between Cities -- calculator
  • Tideschart.com
