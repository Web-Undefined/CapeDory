CDSOA 25th ANNUAL MEETING

Irvington, VA
October 29-31, 2021

Please register by October 1st!

Click Here for additional information, including the registration form.

Members of the Cape Dory Sailboat Owners Association are cordially invited to attend the
25th Annual Meeting in Irvington, VA.

The 2021 Cape Dory Sailboat Owners Association's annual meeting will be held during the weekend of 29-31 October, at the Rappahannock River Yacht Club, Irvington, VA located on on Carters Creek off the Rappahannock River.

If travelling by air, Richmond International Airport is 1 hour 15 minutes by car.  Enterprise, Budget and Avis Car Rental are all located in the terminal.

If travelling by boat, Carters Creek has wonderful anchorages or you can contact the following marinas:

Rappahannock River Yacht Club -- (804) 438-6650

Carters Cove Marina -- (804) 438-5299

Details about this event, travel considerations, and lodging information appear in the document linked above.  Please mark your calendars and let us know if you have any questions.  Please send your registration and check, made out to CDSOA Chesapeake Fleet, using the form accessible from the above link.

We are looking forward to seeing you in Irvington and to a great time at the CDSOA 25th Annual Meeting!

Questions? Contact Katie Potter: Email: edandkatie@aol.com or by phone at 301-642-2816.
