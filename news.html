Free Sailing Magazines

New to Sailing -- Whether you're 7 or 70 you can learn to sail and this web site, including their free online magazine, can help. This is a great tool for getting children started in sailing.

WindCheck Magazine -- This is a free printed and online monthly magazine devoted to sailors and sailing in the Northeast. Pick one up at your marina or yacht club, purchase a subscription, or read it online.

SpinSheet Magazine -- This is also a free printed and online magazine devoted to sailors and sailing on Chesapeake Bay. Pick one up at your marina or yacht club, purchase a subscription, or read it online.

Southwinds Magazine -- A FREE, printed and online monthly Sailing magazine dedicated to Sailing and Cruising in Florida, the Florida Keys, North Carolina, South Carolina, Georgia, Alabama, Mississippi, Louisiana, Texas, The Bahamas and points south.

Latitude 38 -- This is a free printed and online monthly magazine devoted to sailors and sailing in California. Pick one up at your marina or yacht club, purchase a subscription, or read it online.

48° North -- This is a printed and online monthly magazine devoted to sailors and sailing in the Pacific Northwest. Read it online for free.

Great Lakes Scuttlebutt -- This this magazine's focus is on powerboating, sailboaters should be interested in the articles relating to cruising destinations on the Great Lakes. Read it online for free.

Caribbean Compass -- Free online magazine covering cruising destinations, regattas and events in the Caribbean.

Scuttlebutt Sailing News -- This online magazine/web site covers sailing news, especially regarding sail racing events, throughout North America.
