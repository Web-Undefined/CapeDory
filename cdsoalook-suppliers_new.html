Where to Look:
Manufacturers/Suppliers of Original Equipment

For original Cape Dory parts and supplies: The CDSOA Inc. neither endorses nor recommends any of the manufacturers or suppliers listed below. They are listed here for informational purposes. Any questions or problems concerning any of the manufacturers, suppliers or their products should be directed to the individual manufacturer or supplier.

NOTE: The following list is by no means complete (see owners manual for complete equipment list for your boat) but it'll give you a good idea of where to start looking for replacement parts. If you can't find an exact replacement, and let's face it, many items have been discontinued and some manufacturers have gone out of business, CLICK HERE for a list of alternative manufacturers for something close.

BLOCKS:

  • Schaefer Marine

BOTTOM PAINT:

Pre-1980 Cape Dorys sported the following color scheme from the factory: light blue non-skid on the deck and coach roof, red or blue boot stripe [(Boot Top Seminole Red 593, Tarr & Wonson Copper Paint of Gloucester (later by Gloucester Paints then Interlux)); (Boot Top Endeavor Blue 16, Interlux), and blue bottom paint [(Antifouling Blue 1222, Petit) or (Antifouling Blue, EpoxyCop by Rule Industries (later by Interlux)].

Post 1980 Cape Dorys sported the following color scheme from the factory: light tan non-skid on the deck and coach roof, dark brown boot stripe [(Boot Top Dado Dark Brown 246, Interlux)], dark brown cove strip (if present), and dark brown bottom paint [(Antifouling Brown 1522, Petit) or (Antifouling Brown, MicronCSC, Interlux)].

  • Pettit Paints
  • Interlux Yacht Finishes
