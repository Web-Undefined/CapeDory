Member Benefits

Owners Network:

As a CDSOA member you'll belong to a growing network of Cape Dory owners who are more than willing to share their knowledge and experiences concerning their boats and boating, you'll make new friendships that may last a lifetime, and you'll get a chance to meet with the owners and sail on boats that you may aspire to one day own.

Newsletter:

You'll have access to our online newsletter, MASTHEAD. It's usually filled with event information as well as articles submitted by our members regarding their sailing experiences on their Cape Dorys and during rendezvous events.

Events:

In addition to a network of friendly and helpful owners, and our newsletter, we host several rendezvous events and meetings in each of our fleets' regions annualy during the sailing season, as well as a general membership meeting each November.

Fleets:

As a member you choose your level of activity. Whether you just want to surf our web site, converse on the message board or sail to one of the numerous rendezvous, you're sure to find an activity that suits your needs.

We have several very active regional fleets: Carolinas, Central Gulf, Chesapeake, Gulf Coast and Northeast. They all host multiple land and on-the-water events each year. So why not meet up with fellow Cape Dory owners at a rendezvous or float-in. Can't sail there? Why not drive! We usually have a seminar or two as well.

Don't see a Fleet in your area? Why not start one! Each CDSOA Fleet was created "by, for and of" its members. Members make it all possible. Somebody has to take the ball and run with it, why not you?

Web Site:

Member dollars also finance this web site, including "The Cape Dory Message Board", our message board is an invaluable resource for all boaters. If you enjoy our message board, why not join? Member dollars keep it going.

Chandlery:

We are proud of both our organization and our Cape Dorys and we show it by wearing hats and t-shirts or carrying totebag that sport the CDSOA logo. There are other items too. And in addition to the CDSOA logo items, you'll also find links to products offered by other Cape Dory organizations as well as to the web sites for manufacturers, model makers and authors on our More CD Stuff page..
